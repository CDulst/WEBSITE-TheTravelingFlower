practical Info

What is the traveling flower?

The Lady Slipperis an orchid in danger of extinction. The main reason is the change of the climate. We as an organization want to let this plant travel in an ecolocical way to orchid reserve Yachang in China. There he can reproduce and create a new population. The plant is transported by our community who move it from checkpoint to checkpoint in the most ecological way.

How to transport

The route has 24 checkpoints. We are looking for 24 volunteers who want to travel the flower from checkpoint to checkpoint. You can sign up become a carrier. As a carrier, you are responsible that the flower gets to its next checkpoint on the most ecological way

Become a carrier

End destination

Yachang national park is a nature reserve located in Taiwan. It is a place where the orchid can live in it's natural habitat. There the flower can reproduce and start a new population. It's also the end destination of our journey The lady slipper will be planted there, and be a symbolic place for our movement
